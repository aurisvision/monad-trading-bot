## ‚ö†Ô∏è Trading Risks & Safety

### Risk Warnings

**Market Risks**
- **Volatility**: Crypto prices can change rapidly
- **Liquidity Risk**: Some tokens may have limited liquidity
- **Smart Contract Risk**: Potential bugs in token contracts

**Trading Risks**
- **Slippage**: Actual price may differ from expected
- **Front-Running**: MEV bots may affect your trades
- **Gas Costs**: Network fees can be significant

### Safety Measures

**Built-in Protections**
- **Slippage Limits**: Maximum acceptable price movement
- **Gas Limits**: Prevent excessive gas usage
- **Rate Limiting**: Prevent accidental rapid trading
- **Balance Checks**: Ensure sufficient funds before trading

**Best Practices**
- **Start Small**: Begin with small amounts
- **Research Tokens**: Verify token legitimacy
- **Monitor Markets**: Stay informed about market conditions
- **Set Limits**: Define maximum loss tolerance

## üÜò Troubleshooting Trading Issues

### Common Problems

**Transaction Failed**
- **Insufficient Gas**: Increase gas price or limit
- **Slippage Exceeded**: Increase slippage tolerance
- **Network Congestion**: Wait and retry later

**Unexpected Results**
- **Price Impact**: Large trades may move prices significantly
- **Liquidity Changes**: Pool liquidity may have changed
- **Market Movement**: Prices moved during transaction

**Performance Issues**
- **Slow Execution**: Network congestion or low gas
- **Cache Delays**: Data may take time to update
- **Connection Issues**: Check internet and network status
